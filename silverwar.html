The Making Of


Silver War is a first-person shooter where the player is a piece of silverware using tiny guns to dismember zombie food enemies. The cartoony art style mixed with explosive action makes for a fun and exciting playthrough that anybody can enjoy. Silver war is also the most polished project I've ever worked on. With more than 400 hours of effort from myself and two designers, the scope of the game is much larger than others in my collection.
